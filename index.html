At Hare and Edelweiss, we create bespoke, original, luxury pieces of homeware.

We are a family company and share a love of unique design and quality fabrics. Based in Edinburgh, we draw on both city and countryside styles, and are perfectly placed to serve an international market.

About the creators Justine and Jennie

Justine’s passion for design began with altering vintage clothing. As her stitching skills were honed, Justine began to expand by making simple homeware gifts for friends and family, combining her skills and passion until requests for commissions became so numerous the natural conclusion was to set up a business.

Jennie has been creating magical illustrations for her family for over three decades. Her eye for detail and ’magic’ touch have evolved through beautifully decorated children’s nurseries and bedrooms to masterfully illustrated, personalised story books for her own children. As Jennie’s artistic capabilities were recognised, friends and family began to commission her for sketches and paintings of much-loved pets, and for the beautifully painted furniture she had become renowned for. Never one to rest on her laurels, Jennie has immersed herself in designing intricate and charming motifs for our commissions which delight and enchant in equal measure.
